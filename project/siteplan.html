Website Project Site Plan

Author: Jean Ntampaka

Title: Local Cooking Showcase

1. Purpose of the Website

To highlight talented local cooks and their special dishes, making it easy for visitors—especially tourists and food enthusiasts—to discover authentic local cuisine and connect directly with cooks for tasting experiences or private events.

2. Target Audience

  • Tourists visiting the region who are looking for authentic local food
  • Locals interested in discovering home-style meals or catering options
  • Food lovers and culture seekers looking for unique culinary experiences

3. Site Structure (Pages and Descriptions)

Page Description
Home Welcomes users with a brief introduction to the site, its purpose, and highlights a few featured cooks/dishes.
Meet the Cooks A directory or grid of local cooks with brief bios and links to individual profile pages.
Cook Portfolio (Dynamic Page) Each cook’s personal page with photo, bio, specialties, dish images, and contact info.
Dishes Gallery Gallery of featured dishes with names, descriptions, and filtering by category (via JavaScript).
Contact General contact form and links/forms to reach cooks directly; may include social links.

4. Features and Functionality

  • Responsive Design using HTML and CSS (mobile-first)
  • JavaScript Interactivity:
    • Dynamic dish filtering by category
    • Interactive gallery lightbox
    • Toggle "Read more" for cook bios
  • Clean, modern layout with a warm, community-focused aesthetic
